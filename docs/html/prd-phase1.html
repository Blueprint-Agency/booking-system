Section 03

Client Experience

A seamless journey from discovering a session to attending it — designed to feel effortless on any device.

Finding & Booking a Session

Clients see available sessions in a calendar view or scrollable list. They can filter by category, instructor, or level.

  • Calendar view — weekly/monthly calendar showing session times at a glance
  • List view — a scrollable list, ideal for mobile browsing
  • Smart filters — narrow by category, instructor / staff member, or level
  • Real-time availability — see exactly how many spots remain
  • Waitlist — join automatically when a session is full; get notified when a spot opens

Booking Flow

One to two clicks to book. No unnecessary steps, forms, or confirmations.

Paying for Sessions

Flexible payment options to suit every client. All payments processed securely via Stripe.

  • Pay per session — single payment at the time of booking
  • Session packages — buy a bundle (e.g., 5 or 10 sessions) and use them over time
  • Monthly memberships — recurring subscription for unlimited or set number of sessions per month

My Account

Everything a client needs, all in one place.

  • Upcoming bookings — see what's scheduled, with session details
  • Cancel or reschedule — within the business's cancellation policy window
  • Package balance — see how many sessions remain in a purchased package
  • History — past bookings, attendance records, and payment receipts
  • Invoices — download or review any invoice from previous purchases

Digital Waiver

First-time onboarding — handled once, stored permanently.

  • The waiver form appears automatically during the first booking or account setup
  • Clients read and e-sign on their device
  • The signed waiver is stored securely under their profile
  • Admin can access it at any time for compliance
No paper forms, no scanning — it's handled once and stored permanently.
Section 04

Admin Experience

Your operational command center — manage sessions, clients, staff, and finances from a single dashboard.

Session & Schedule Management

Create and manage your full schedule from one place.

  • Create sessions with name, type, level, duration, instructor, and price
  • Recurring schedules — e.g., "Beginner Class every Monday and Wednesday at 9am"
  • Capacity limits — set maximum clients per session
  • Waitlist rules — enable/disable, set maximum waitlist size
  • Late-entry rules — configurable cutoff (e.g., 10 minutes after session starts)
  • Workshops & events — one-off sessions with custom pricing and capacity
Changes to the schedule are reflected immediately on the client booking site.

Booking Management

See who's in every session and manage bookings hands-on.

  • Session rosters — full list of booked clients for any session
  • Manual add/remove — handle phone bookings, walk-ins, or removals
  • Automated waitlist — cancelled spots are automatically filled and clients notified
  • Attendance status — mark each client as Attended, Late, or No-Show

Client CRM

A complete profile for every client, all in one place.

  • Contact details — name, email, phone number
  • Package status — active packages, remaining sessions, expiry dates
  • Membership status — current plan, renewal date
  • Attendance history — full record of sessions attended, late arrivals, and no-shows
  • Notes — private notes about health considerations, preferences, special accommodations

QR Code Check-In

Each client has a unique QR code in their account, accessible on their phone.

  1. Client arrives and shows their QR code on their phone
  2. Front-desk staff scans it using any device with a camera
  3. System confirms check-in and records attendance
  4. If arrival exceeds the late cutoff (default: 10 min), check-in is blocked

Instructor Management

Manage your teaching team and track what they're owed.

  • Assign instructors to specific sessions
  • View schedules — each instructor's upcoming sessions at a glance
  • Compensation tracking — the system calculates and displays earnings

Staff Management

Manage your team beyond instructors.

  • Staff accounts — create accounts for front-desk staff, managers, etc.
  • Roles & permissions — control what each staff member can see and do
  • Leave management — submit, approve, or reject leave requests
  • Calendar visibility — see who's working on any given day

Sales Tracking

Know who's selling and how much.

  • Sales by staff member — see which team member sold what
  • Commission calculation — automatic calculation based on your rules, displayed in reports

Payments & Packages

Full control over your pricing and revenue.

  • Single session prices — set per-session rates
  • Session packages — bundles like 5 or 10 sessions at a discounted rate
  • Monthly memberships — recurring billing for regular clients
  • Transaction history — all payments, filterable by date, client, or type

Invoices

Every purchase automatically generates a professional invoice.

  • Sent to the client's email immediately after payment
  • Stored in the client's account for future reference
  • Accessible to admin at any time
  • Includes: date, description, amount paid, payment method

Dashboard & Analytics

A real-time overview of your business's performance.

  • Session occupancy rate — how full are your sessions? Which are consistently under-booked?
  • Revenue tracking — daily, weekly, and monthly revenue trends
  • Active vs. inactive clients — who's booking vs. who hasn't visited recently
  • Package sales performance — which packages sell best, revenue by type
Section 05

Configurable Business Policies

The system ships with sensible defaults, but everything can be adjusted to match your business's way of working.

Cancellation Policy

  • Set a cancellation window (e.g., 12 hours before a session)
  • Define late-cancel consequences (session deducted or penalty fee)
  • Choose whether late cancellations count as no-shows

No-Show Policy

  • Track no-shows automatically via attendance records
  • Set consequences (e.g., warning or booking restriction after 3 no-shows)

Waitlist Rules

  • Enable or disable per session
  • Set maximum waitlist size
  • Automatic promotion when a spot opens

Late-Entry Cutoff

  • Set the grace period (default: 10 minutes)
  • After cutoff, clients cannot check in
  • Late arrivals past cutoff are marked as no-shows

Real-Time

Clear Feedback

Every action gives immediate, clear confirmation — on screen and via email.

Clean & Modern

A professional, uncluttered interface that reflects well on your brand.
